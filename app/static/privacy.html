Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025.04.17

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your music accounts, we collect the following data:

Spotify

  • App Client ID & Secret (used server-side for API access)
  • Access Token (to retrieve your recently played tracks)

Yandex Music

  • Yandex Token (used to retrieve your recently played tracks)

We do not collect or store any other personal information.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected tokens only to:

  • Access your recently played or currently playing music
  • Generate a message or preview to share on Telegram

We do not use your data for analytics, advertising, tracking, or any other purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Tokens are stored securely and encrypted on our server.
  • We do not share your information with any third parties.
  • Only essential server processes have access to these tokens.

4. Data Retention

  • Tokens are retained only as long as you use the bot.
  • You can delete your stored data at any time by messaging the bot with the command /logout.

5. Third-Party Services

We rely on the official APIs of:

  • Spotify
  • Yandex Music

Please review their respective privacy policies for more information on how they handle your data.
We also comply with telegram Standard Privacy Policy for Bots and Mini Apps.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your stored data
  • Revoke access at any time via Spotify or Yandex settings
  • Request permanent deletion of your data by messaging /logout to the bot
